About Stoic Companion

Stoic Companion is a reflective web app that distills timeless Stoic wisdom into serene, thoughtful guidance. In moments of doubt or search for clarity, it speaks with the enlightenment of ancient minds. The app is a RAG system powered by Gemini LLM, it offers responses both rational and calm—a silent companion walking beside you on your journey to self.

Tech Stack Used

  • Python
  • LangChain
  • Gemini LLM
  • HTML/CSS/JavaScript
  • FAISS vectorstore
  • BeautifulSoup
  • Embeddings from HuggingFace
